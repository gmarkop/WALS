About WALS

The World Atlas of Language Structures (WALS) is a comprehensive database of structural (phonological, grammatical, lexical) properties of languages gathered from descriptive materials. This local explorer allows you to browse and analyze WALS data on your own computer.

Quick Start

  • Browse Languages: Explore over 2,500 languages with detailed information
  • Explore Features: Discover 192 typological features across 11 linguistic areas
  • View Map: See the geographic distribution of languages
  • Search: Find specific languages or features quickly

Featured Topics

Phonology

Consonant and vowel inventories, syllable structure

Morphology
